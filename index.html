You are given 2 cards and $100 at the beginning of the game. Your get blackjack by getting 21 points. If the sum of your cards is less than 21, you can draw a new card. If your point exceeds 21, you lose the game. Everytime you lose a game of Blackjack, you lose $20. If you have $0 left, you are out of the game.

2-10 represents their own value

Ace (A) = 11

King, Queen and Jack = 10
