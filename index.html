You are given 2 cards at the beginning of the game. Your goal is to get 21 points. If the sum of your cards is less than 21, you can draw a new card. If your point exceeds 21, you are out of the game.

2-10 represents their own value.

Ace (A) = 11

King, Queen and Jack = 10
